Health-Tech AI

AI Shopping Assistant (Verifyr)

Problem

Fitness & health enthusiasts are overwhelmed and 67% experience decision paralysis from information overload, contradictory reviews, complex tech specs, and commission-biased recommendations.

Approach

Built conversational Health-Tech advisor that guides users through wearable selection via smart questions, compares 3-4 devices side-by-side, provides neutral recommendations, review curation, and Q&A support in one personalized flow - from needs analysis to pre and post-purchase support. 100% commission-free for unbiased recommendations.

Execution

Led discovery and user research (n=4) for AI Health-Tech shopping advisor, identifying critical pain points: 67% experience decision paralysis and lack trust in recommendations for technical products. Validated market opportunity for e-commerce smart wearables with SAM of €1.8B, SOM of 9M€ targeting 0.5% market share through 5% commission model, projecting €450K+ annual revenue potential. Defined comprehensive product strategy including Business Value Proposition, competitive analysis, Lean assumptions, technical constraints, OKRs, and North Star Metric (MAU) as foundation for MVP development alongside Go-to-Market strategy.

Designed conversational AI flow prototypes in Miro and Figma for early UX validation before development investment, reducing design risk through 4 iterative rounds of user testing and mentor feedback. Validated product assumptions via landing page demand test, achieving 8% conversion rate (16/200 visitors submitted email), confirming market interest for MVP development. Wrote and prioritized 40+ user stories with acceptance criteria in JIRA based on user value and business outcomes, structuring product backlog for efficient sprint planning across 3 sprints with clear development priorities.

Local RAG System

E-Commerce QA Bot

Problem

Online shoppers waste hours jumping between product pages, review sites, and forums to make confident purchase decisions. They need unbiased guidance based on actual product data and verified curated reviews - all in one place.

Approach

Built product-specific knowledge bot retrieving answers from product specifications, user manuals, setup guides, and verified test results. Pre-purchase support helps users compare recommended products, clarify technical details, translate complex specs into understandable benefits, and make confident purchase decisions. Post-purchase support guides initial setup, configuration, feature usage, and troubleshooting - transforming product documentation into on-demand assistance.

Execution

Implemented production-ready RAG pipeline with multi-model LLM integration (Claude, GPT) for cost-quality optimization. Built FastAPI backend with Langfuse observability for real-time tracking. Established RAGAS evaluation framework (87% faithfulness, 85% relevance) for continuous iteration and quality monitoring.

Outcome: Delivers accurate, source-backed answers from 250+ pages of product documentation in 3-7 seconds. Reduced research time from hours to seconds in pilot with 2 products.

AI in Automotive Infotainment

GenAI Feature(s) in CarPlay

Problem

Launching innovative GenAI features required early validation with real users. Traditional approach: iterate until 100% perfect before rollout - slow, expensive, risky. Needed structured method to validate features early, gather feedback, and de-risk investment before full production.

Approach

Beta Program Framework: Structured approach enabling real customers to provide direct feedback on pre-production features through time-limited rollouts. Dual goals: (1) Validate features to improve product quality before production, (2) Create communication potential through innovation. Designed UX journeys, onboarding flows, and feedback mechanisms. Leveraged Community of 300+ members actively participating in product development while gaining early access to exclusive GenAI features.

Execution

Coordinated cross-functional teams across Business Owners, Marketing, UX, QA, Legal, and Product. Conducted competitor analysis and created user stories with UX journey maps. Collaborated with Quality and Marketing stakeholders to define success criteria: speech quality, content accuracy, and feature availability. Built comprehensive KPI framework tracking Adoption Rate, Usage Frequency, Customer Satisfaction, Session Duration, and Abandonment Rate. Established technical quality gates including Latency, Error Rate, Accuracy, and Safety Score (99% harmful content filtered). Developed cost estimation model covering API usage, infrastructure, and development resources. Implemented multi-phase rollout (internal → power users → broader audience) with structured feedback collection and weekly iteration cycles.

Pilot: Voice Stories Feature

Feature: Location-aware GenAI generating audio stories about nearby sights using ChatGPT + Voice.AI integration.

Community: 100 selected real customers providing direct feedback on pre-production feature across USA, DE+UK, China, Japan/South Korea. Time-limited rollout: <6 months | Budget: ~1M€

Customer Benefits: Early access to exclusive location-aware AI feature + active participation in shaping production version as enthusiastic Community members.

Outcome:To achieve dual goals - validate product quality across 4+ markets and create communication potential through innovation leadership by building a reusable framework for future Digital & GenAI features. Beta Program transformed customers into co-creators and brand advocates. Enabled early go/no-go decisions: collect feedback before full rollout or kill product when adoption rate insufficient - preventing wasted production investment.
